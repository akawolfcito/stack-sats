# Privacy Policy (DenVault)

Effective date: 2025-01-04

DenVault is a DenLabs project.

> **Official Policy URL:** https://akawolfcito.github.io/stack-sats/privacy.html

## Contact

- Privacy: wolfcito.learn+privacy@gmail.com
- Security: wolfcito.learn+security@gmail.com

## Scope

This policy describes how DenVault handles data in:

1. The browser extension (“Extension”)
2. The companion web front-end (“App”), if applicable
3. This repository and its documentation

## Data We Do Not Intend to Collect

DenLabs does not intend to collect:

- Your seed phrase / mnemonic
- Your private keys
- Your passwords

If you discover behavior inconsistent with this, contact Security immediately.

## Data Processed Locally

The Extension may process locally (on your device):

- Wallet addresses and public keys derived from your seed phrase
- Transaction details you choose to sign
- Local settings (e.g., selected account, network)

Local storage is controlled by the browser environment. If you uninstall the extension, browser-managed storage may be removed
depending on your browser settings.

## Network Requests / Third-Party Services

To function, DenVault may communicate with public blockchain infrastructure (e.g., RPC endpoints, API providers).
When you use those endpoints, those providers may receive:

- Your IP address
- Request metadata and timing
- Blockchain addresses and transaction data (which may be public on-chain)

These providers operate under their own privacy policies.

## Telemetry

Unless explicitly enabled in the product, DenVault does not include intentional analytics/telemetry collection.
If telemetry is added in the future, we will:

- Document what is collected
- Provide an opt-in or clear controls where feasible
- Update this policy

## Chrome Web Store / Extension Store Disclosures

DenVault is intended to operate as a non-custodial wallet. Users are responsible for safeguarding their seed phrase and device security.

## Children’s Privacy

DenVault is not directed to children and should be used only by users who can understand wallet security responsibilities.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date and repository history.
