SIP-010 Token Support

View all your fungible tokens following the SIP-010 standard with automatic metadata fetching.

dApp Connections

Connect securely to Stacks dApps using the @stacks/connect v8 standard and WBIP protocols.

Encrypted Storage

Your seed phrase is encrypted with AES-256-GCM and protected by a 6-digit PIN. Never transmitted externally.
